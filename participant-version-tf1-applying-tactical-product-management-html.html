What does Owning the End-to-End Product Journey mean?

Owning the end-to-end product journey involves taking full responsibility and being held 

accountable for the complete lifecycle of a product, from initial concept through 

development and launch

What aspects does this include?

Customer-Centricity 

Teams actively engage with customers, leveraging feedback to continuously reﬁne 

strategy and priorities to drive maximum value and satisfaction

Ownership and Empowerment

Teams foster cross-functional collaboration across teams through clear RACI 

frameworks, driving shared ownership of product success

Accountability 

Teams at GP own outcomes across all stages of the product life cycle, ensuring 

strategic alignment and focus on value delivery
