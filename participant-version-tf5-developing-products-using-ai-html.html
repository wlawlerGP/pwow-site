Key Takeaways

1.

AI enhances how PMs execute across the PDLC: It improves speed, clarity, 

and decision quality when applied intentionally

4.

AI supports decisions but accountability stays with the PM: AI should 

inform thinking, not replace judgment

2.

Capability and autonomy deﬁne AI’s role: Every AI use case varies by 

capability (Perceive, Predict, Generate) and level of autonomy (Automation, 

Augmentation, Agency)  

3.

Responsible AI use requires guardrails and veriﬁcation: Bias, hallucination, 

and compliance risks should be considered and mitigated
